How Jacks's Music Taste Has Impacted Me

Jack’s music taste, as he describes it, is centered on the emotions a song evokes and its overall message. He often mentions that many of the country songs he listens to aren’t overly complex in their production or lyricism but are straightforward and impactful. I find myself drawn to this type of music when I’m feeling homesick. It carries a nostalgic quality for me, and Jack shares a similar sentiment. He says the genre is comforting, reminding him of home—a small farm town where we both grew up. I think his music taste influences me the most when I’m searching for a slow, sad song that brings a sense of home. He has an excellent playlist of sad country songs, and when I need a track that truly tugs at my heartstrings, I find folk and country music deliver that emotional connection best.
